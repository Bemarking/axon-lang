## Why a CLA?

axon-lang is published under the GNU Affero General Public License
v3.0-or-later (AGPL-3.0+) — see [`LICENSE`](./LICENSE). The AGPL is a
strong copyleft license that protects the commons: forks deployed as
network services must release their modifications.

Bemarking ALSO maintains a commercial product, **axon-enterprise**,
that embeds axon-lang and is distributed to commercial customers under
a proprietary End User License Agreement. For Bemarking to embed
axon-lang in axon-enterprise without forcing every axon-enterprise
customer to comply with AGPL, Bemarking must hold a separate
commercial license to the axon-lang code. The CLA grants Bemarking
that license PROSPECTIVELY for every contribution — your contribution
stays AGPL-licensed for the public AND becomes commercially
sublicensable through Bemarking.

This dual-license pattern is used by many infrastructure-grade
open-source projects (MongoDB pre-2018, Elastic prior to SSPL,
Grafana Loki, CockroachDB, MariaDB, Qt, etc.). The CLA is the
standard legal instrument that makes the pattern work.

The full architecture is documented in
[the axon-enterprise `LICENSING.md`](https://github.com/Bemarking/axon-enterprise/blob/master/LICENSING.md).

## C. How to sign

The first time You open a pull request against `Bemarking/axon-lang`,
the [cla-assistant.io](https://cla-assistant.io/) GitHub App will
post a comment on Your PR with a signing link. Click the link,
authenticate with GitHub, and confirm that You have read and agree to
this CLA.

Your signature is recorded against:

- Your GitHub username
- The email address(es) associated with Your commits
- The timestamp of the signature
- The PR number where You first signed

The signature carries forward to all future Contributions; You do
not need to re-sign for subsequent PRs unless this CLA's text changes
materially (in which case the bot will request re-signature).

If You contribute through multiple GitHub accounts (e.g., personal +
work), each account must sign separately.

If You prefer to sign offline (without using the CLA-assistant bot),
please email a signed PDF copy of this CLA to
[legal@bemarking.com.co](mailto:legal@bemarking.com.co) with the
subject line "axon-lang CLA — [Your full legal name]" and identify
the GitHub account(s) the signature applies to.
